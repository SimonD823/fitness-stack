HOW TO USE
================================================================================
1. Complete your workout
2. Open Garmin Connect (web or app) → find the activity → Edit
3. Copy the relevant template above into the Description/Notes field
4. Fill in your actual reps and weights (replace the @ placeholders)
5. Save — the next garmin_direct_sync cycle will pick up the notes and write
   the correct exercise names and weights to InfluxDB automatically

Notes take priority over Garmin's built-in exercise tracking, so even if
Garmin misnames an exercise, the notes version will be used in InfluxDB
and the daily brief coaching analysis.
